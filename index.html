Bio

I'm a front-end developer and user experience designer living in the city. I care about how people sense the world, learn about the world and play in the world. So, I try to make things pretty, cook yummy food and spend time with great games.

My previous career was technical recruiting/account managing at a small company, but I wasn't able to combine sense, learning and play, so I quit. The next iteration of my career will combine these wonderful things!

I really enjoy games, dining, adventure, sports and travel, but I'm constantly having my mind captured by novel concepts from other fields.
