Bio

My previous career was technical recruiting/account managing at a small company. It had an energetic, team-focused environment, but it was all about the commission. I am looking for the next iteration of my career to be completely different and much the same--"all-hands-on-deck" continues as my motto and helping one another as a given across the team are both incredibly important.
The difference is that I want my team to build something fantastic, and I know I need to assume the weight of making this happen. The product is only outstanding if it is designed the right way--I am growing to be that designer. I do really enjoy dining, adventure, sports and travel, but I'm more interested in working on the greatest idea I can than finding the best idea in a particular field.
