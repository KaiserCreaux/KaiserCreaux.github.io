Welcome to Polly.

Politics is broken, and it's time to do something about it.

  • 91% of Congress gets re-elected despite a 10 percent approval rating.
  • Your representative likely spends 30-70% of their time fund raising: calling wealthy people asking for money.
  • Only 27% of Americans can name both of their senators.

We know democracy is important, but our government can't seem to get anything done.

Let us never forget that government is ourselves and not an alien power over us. The ultimate rulers of our democracy are not a President and senators and congressmen and government officials, but the voters of this country.

— Franklin D. Roosevelt.

At Polly, We create simple tools that make average people like you more powerful, and we make the tools free and widely available to everyone.

Democracy is based upon the conviction that there are extraordinary possibilities in ordinary people.

— Harry Emerson Fosdick

Our first tool connects you to the legislative process in a way that wasn't possible in the past. To begin, we'll let you know who is representing you in Congress. We'll also give you a new feed of their voting history, and even notify you when they vote on a new bill. It's like Instagram with impact. Sign in now to see your representatives.

About Us

  1. We are toolmakers. We don't have an agenda. We are independent and nonpartisan. We build tools to make people more powerful. This takes dedication, iteration, and the ability to take pleasure in helping others.
  2. We have impact. We pick the biggest problems and solve them. This requires us to deeply understand a problem, and to be unafraid of shaking up the status quo.
  3. We are open. Movements are not built through secrecy. We build trust inside and outside the organization through openness and transparency. This isn't just lip service. See How We Work or view our backlog on Trello.
